Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know your services will actually deliver results?

That’s a fair question and honestly, it’s the most important one. When working with Madisyn Shi, the focus is on delivering measurable outcomes. Every website is built for conversion, and every ad campaign is backed by strategy, testing, and optimization. I use proven frameworks and continuous tracking to improve results over time.

What makes you different from other web designers and ads marketers?

Most freelancers focus on either design or marketing. Madisyn Shi combines both, ensuring your website not only looks good but converts visitors into customers. This integrated approach delivers stronger results.

How long will it take to see results?

Websites show immediate improvement in design and user experience. Ads typically generate data within 3–7 days, with optimization and scaling happening over 2–4 weeks.

Do you work with all types of businesses?

I mainly work with e-commerce brands, startups, and service businesses focused on growth. The most important factor is your commitment to quality and long-term success.

What do you need before starting?

I’ll need your business details, goals, and any available assets like logo or content. Don’t worry—I’ll guide you through everything.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Revisions are part of the process to ensure the final result meets your expectations and business goals.

How do you handle communication?

You’ll receive regular updates, and I remain available for questions throughout the project.

Can you guarantee sales or ROI?

No one can guarantee exact results. However, I ensure a structured, data-driven approach to maximize performance.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. I provide continued support for both websites and ad campaigns depending on your needs.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out via contact options and share your project details. We’ll discuss and move forward professionally.

My Process

01. Confirmation

Once you reach out, we start with a discussion to fully understand your business, goals, and expectations. This ensures alignment on scope, timeline, and outcomes.

02. Contract Agreement

A clear agreement is created outlining scope, deliverables, timeline, and terms to ensure transparency and protect both sides.

03. Payment (Credit)

A deposit or full payment is required to secure your project slot and allow proper resource allocation.

04. Project Commencement

Work begins with research, planning, and execution tailored to your needs—whether it’s a website or ad campaign.

05. Completion & Delivery

You receive the final deliverables, and any agreed revisions are handled to ensure satisfaction.

06. Ongoing Support

Support continues after completion, with guidance, updates, and long-term collaboration when needed.
